# Heartbeat checklist

Jarvis works this list quietly in the background (off the voice hot path). It
speaks up **only** when something here is genuinely worth interrupting for —
otherwise it stays silent. Keep items few and high-signal; noise trains people to
ignore proactive nudges.

- Anything time-sensitive in the next hour that the user hasn't acknowledged?
- A clear, important change since the last check worth a one-line heads-up?

(When in doubt, say nothing.)
